R&R at Econometric Theory, 2026

A Relaxation Approach to Synthetic Control

Chengwang Liao, Zhentao Shi, Yapeng Zheng

We develop a relaxation approach to synthetic control, which allows for consistent estimation of the weights and average treatment effects on the treated unit when the donor pool is large.

R&R at Journal of Econometrics, 2026

Nickell Meets Stambaugh: A Tale of Two Biases in Panel Predictive Regressions

Chengwang Liao, Ziwei Mei, Zhentao Shi

This paper describes the coexistence of the Nickell bias and the Stambaugh bias in panel predictive regressions when the predictor is highly persistent, and introduces an innovative estimator to effectively remove this composite bias and reinstate standard inferential procedures.
